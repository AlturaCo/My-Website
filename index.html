The Process

How it works.

01

Meta Ads reach the right parents & students

We build targeted campaigns on Facebook and Instagram that reach families actively searching for college counseling help — by grade level, geography, interest, and intent signal. We write the creative, run the tests, and cut spend on anything that doesn’t convert.

02

Our funnel & booking system turns clicks into consultations

Every ad leads to a dedicated landing page built to convert. Interested families fill out an intake form, qualify themselves, and book directly into your calendar — with automated reminders that cut no-shows before they happen.

03

Automated follow-up keeps them warm until they enroll

Leads who don’t book immediately enter an email and SMS follow-up sequence that stays in touch automatically. Warm families are nurtured until they’re ready — so you close more of the interest you already paid to generate.
