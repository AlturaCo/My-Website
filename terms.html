1. Services

Altura Co provides student acquisition services for college counseling businesses, including paid advertising management (Meta Ads), conversion funnel and landing page builds, lead nurture and follow-up automation, consultation booking systems, reputation and referral programs, and monthly performance reporting. The specific scope, deliverables, and timelines for each engagement are set out in a separate Agreement or proposal accepted by the Client.

2. Engagement Structures

We offer two engagement structures, the specific terms of which are agreed privately with each client and set out in a separate written Agreement or proposal:

  • Option A — Performance Only: No upfront retainer. The Agency earns a fee based on students acquired through tracked agency channels. A minimum commitment period applies. A buyout clause is available. The Client funds all advertising spend separately.
  • Option B — Retainer Plus Performance: A fixed retainer for an initial period plus a performance component on acquired students. After the initial period, the retainer and performance component adjust as specified in the Agreement. The Client funds all advertising spend separately.

Specific fees, performance share percentages, retainer amounts, and commitment lengths are not published publicly and are discussed directly with prospective clients. All commercial terms are confirmed in a written Agreement before work begins. Existing Agreements will not be modified without written consent from both parties.

3. The Performance Guarantee

Both engagement options include a performance guarantee: if the Agency’s work does not generate attributed revenue at least equal to the Client’s total investment with the Agency over the guarantee period, the Client pays no further Agency fees until that benchmark is met.

The guarantee is subject to the following conditions:

  • The Client maintains an active advertising budget throughout the engagement period as agreed in the written Agreement.
  • The Client provides timely access to required assets: ad accounts, website or landing page infrastructure, CRM, email platform, and any other platforms specified in the Agreement.
  • The Client has a proven, operating counseling service with positive results and testimonials from past students.
  • Attribution tracking is operational and agreed upon before the guarantee period begins.
  • The definition of the Client’s “investment” and the method for measuring attributed revenue are confirmed in writing before the engagement begins.

The guarantee does not apply if underperformance results from factors within the Client’s sole control, including service quality issues, withdrawal of ad spend, or failure to provide required access or materials.

4. Attribution and Performance Calculation

All revenue counted toward the Agency’s performance metrics must arrive through dedicated landing pages, unique tracking links, or tracked booking flows that the Agency controls and that are specified in the Agreement. Revenue arriving through channels not controlled by the Agency is excluded from performance calculations. Attribution rules are agreed in writing before engagement begins. The Agency provides monthly reporting showing attributed revenue and performance calculations. The Client has 14 days from receipt of each report to raise a dispute; disputes not raised within 14 days are deemed accepted.

5. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to all required platforms, accounts, and credentials.
  • Maintain the advertising spend specified in the Agreement throughout the engagement.
  • Respond to Agency requests within a reasonable timeframe (generally 3–5 business days).
  • Maintain adequate service quality, delivery standards, and client communication.
  • Ensure all services and marketing materials comply with applicable laws and platform policies.
  • Notify the Agency promptly of any material changes to the business.

The Agency’s performance obligations and any guarantees are conditional on the Client meeting these responsibilities. If the Client fails to do so, the Agency may suspend work or terminate the Agreement with written notice.

6. Advertising Spend

Advertising spend is funded directly by the Client and is separate from any retainer or performance payments to the Agency. The Agency manages campaigns on the Client’s behalf but takes no margin on ad spend. The Client is solely responsible for funding ad accounts and for all charges incurred on those accounts.

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any non-public information received from the other party, including business strategies, financial data, student metrics, and proprietary systems. 9. Term and Termination

Engagements have a minimum commitment of three months from the date the first deliverable is launched. After the minimum period, either party may terminate with 30 days’ written notice. The Agency may terminate immediately if: the Client fails to pay within 14 days of a written reminder; the Client materially breaches these Terms and fails to remedy within 14 days; or the Client engages in illegal or fraudulent activities. Upon termination, the Client must pay all amounts accrued to the termination date.

10. Post-Termination Performance Obligations

Students acquired through Agency-controlled channels during the engagement continue to generate performance fee obligations after termination, for 12 months from the date of acquisition or until the buyout clause is exercised. Buyout terms, where applicable, are specified in the relevant Agreement.
