Services

Everything your business needs to acquire students.

We don’t pick one lever and pull it. We build the entire acquisition system — ads, funnel, follow-up, and booking — because driving traffic into a business that can’t convert or follow up just wastes budget.

Core Services

Six services. One integrated system.

Every service is designed to compound with the others. We build them in a specific sequence because order matters — turning on paid ads before the funnel and follow-up are in place means most of that spend is wasted.

📣

01

Student Acquisition (Meta Ads)

We manage end-to-end paid acquisition on Meta (Facebook and Instagram). Starting with creative concepts tailored to parents and students searching for counseling, we produce ad content that speaks directly to their concerns — getting into the right school, standing out in a competitive pool, not wasting the application season. We test multiple concepts simultaneously, cut losers, scale winners, and layer in retargeting campaigns once the funnel is proven.

Retargeting parents who visited your landing page but didn’t book converts at significantly higher rates than cold audiences. It is typically the most immediately available improvement for businesses that have never implemented it.

What’s Included
  • ✓ Paid social campaign management (Meta / Facebook / Instagram)
  • ✓ Creative development and multi-concept testing
  • ✓ Retargeting campaigns for site visitors
  • ✓ Audience research & targeting strategy
  • ✓ Continuous spend optimisation
  • ✓ Attribution tracking setup
🖥️

02

Conversion Funnel & Landing Pages

The typical counseling business website lists credentials and describes the service. It doesn’t communicate specific outcomes for students, build trust through social proof from past families, or make the next step obvious. Parents land, browse, and leave without booking a consultation.

We build a dedicated landing page and application funnel — separate from your main website — designed to convert a parent who sees your ad into a booked consultation. We also simplify the path from interest to booking so fewer leads fall through the cracks.

What We Build
  • ✓ Dedicated acquisition landing page
  • ✓ Conversion-focused copy & trust architecture
  • ✓ Testimonial integration from past students and families
  • ✓ Clear call-to-action to book a consultation
  • ✓ Intake form and pre-qualification flow
  • ✓ Dedicated attribution landing pages per campaign
✉️

03

Lead Nurture & Follow-Up Automation

Most counseling businesses have no follow-up after an initial inquiry. If a parent fills out a contact form and you don’t respond the same day, the chance of booking drops dramatically. We build email and SMS sequences inside your CRM that follow up automatically — immediately, then at 24 hours, 72 hours, and beyond — until they book or explicitly opt out.

Once built, the system runs permanently without ongoing intervention. Every lead is followed up with, every time.

What We Build
  • ✓ Immediate inquiry response automation
  • ✓ Multi-day follow-up email + SMS sequences
  • ✓ Lead re-engagement for cold inquiries
  • ✓ CRM setup and contact management
  • ✓ Pipeline tracking from inquiry to enrolled student
📅

04

Consultation Booking System

Even when parents are interested, a friction-heavy booking process kills conversions. Confusing availability calendars, back-and-forth emails to find a time, and no confirmation or reminder emails all push warm leads toward competitors who make it easier.

We set up streamlined calendar booking integrated with your workflow, automated qualification so you only meet with serious families, and confirmation and reminder emails that reduce no-shows and keep your schedule full.

What We Set Up
  • ✓ Calendar booking tool integration
  • ✓ Qualification step before booking
  • ✓ Confirmation email and automated reminders (24h and 1h before)
  • ✓ No-show follow-up sequence
  • ✓ Booking flow integrated with your CRM
⭐

05

Reputation & Referral Engine

Happy families who enrolled students with your business are your most powerful acquisition channel — but only if you have a system to activate them. We build a review collection sequence targeting recent success stories and a referral program that gives past families an easy way to recommend you, then rewards them when a new student enrolls.

This runs alongside paid acquisition so both channels compound over time. Organic reviews and referrals also lower your cost per student from paid ads by building credibility at the top of your funnel.

What We Build
  • ✓ Review request email sequence targeting recent success stories
  • ✓ Referral program with unique tracking links
  • ✓ Referral reward structure
  • ✓ Reputation management across key platforms
  • ✓ Monthly referral performance tracking

Runs permanently once built. Compounds alongside paid acquisition over time.

📊

06

Reporting & Optimisation

Each month you receive a clear performance report covering the metrics that actually matter: consultations booked through our channels, students enrolled and attributed to our system, cost per consultation booked, show rate, close rate, and the month-over-month trend on all of the above.

We use the same data to continuously optimise — pausing underperforming ad sets, improving the follow-up sequences where leads are going cold, testing landing page variations, and identifying where in the funnel families are dropping out.

Metrics We Track
  • ✓ Consultations booked via agency channels
  • ✓ New students enrolled and attributed
  • ✓ Cost per consultation booked
  • ✓ Consultation show rate and close rate
  • ✓ Source breakdown (paid, referral, organic)
  • ✓ Month-over-month trend on all above

Delivered monthly. Used to drive continuous optimisation across all six services.
