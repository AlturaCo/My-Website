Services

Everything your subscriber journey needs.

Core Services

Six services. One integrated system.

Every service is designed to compound with the others. We build them in a specific sequence because order matters — driving traffic into a leaky funnel just burns budget faster.

📣

01

Subscriber Acquisition

We manage end-to-end paid acquisition across social platforms. Starting with raw unboxing footage from your subscribers, we produce short-form video ads that consistently outperform professionally produced content in this category. We test 3–5 concepts simultaneously, cut losers, scale winners, and layer in retargeting campaigns once the funnel is proven.

Retargeting visitors who didn’t subscribe converts at 2–3× the rate of cold traffic at a lower cost per acquisition — it’s the most immediately available improvement most brands have never implemented.

What’s Included
  • ✓ Video ad creative from existing subscriber footage
  • ✓ Paid social campaign management
  • ✓ Multi-concept creative testing
  • ✓ Retargeting campaigns
  • ✓ Audience research & targeting strategy
  • ✓ Continuous spend optimisation

Ad spend is funded directly by the client. Minimum ~$2,000/mo recommended.

✉️

02

Email & Retention Systems

This is where the majority of client impact concentrates. We build six automated sequences that together form the retention backbone of your business. Most clients have only a welcome email when we arrive. We build what’s missing and make it run permanently without ongoing intervention.

Annual plan upsells alone reduce churn by roughly 51%. Cancel-save flows save 15–25% of would-be cancellations. These are not incremental improvements — they compound month over month.

Sequences We Build
  • ✓ 90-day onboarding & brand relationship sequence
  • ✓ Failed-payment (dunning) sequence — 4–5 emails over 7 days
  • ✓ Cancel-save flow with pause/skip/discount options
  • ✓ Win-back at 30, 60 & 90 days post-cancellation
  • ✓ Annual-plan upsell at 60 & 90 day marks
  • ✓ Referral activation for happiest subscribers
🖥️

03

Landing Page Builds

The typical subscription landing page is a product description with a subscribe button. It fails at every critical moment: it doesn’t communicate specific value, build trust through social proof, neutralise the top objections, or make the annual plan feel like the obvious choice.

We rebuild it from the ground up. We also simplify the checkout flow and add an annual-plan upsell immediately before purchase completion — consistently one of the highest-ROI interventions we make.

What We Rebuild
  • ✓ Full subscription landing page rebuild
  • ✓ Conversion-focused copy & trust architecture
  • ✓ Testimonial integration & objection neutralisation
  • ✓ Checkout simplification
  • ✓ Annual-plan upsell at checkout
  • ✓ Dedicated attribution landing pages per channel
🛡️

04

Failed Payment Recovery

Around 68% of all subscription churn is involuntary — a card declines, the subscriber doesn’t notice, and they’re gone. Almost no small brand has a system to recover these. We configure automated card-retry logic inside your existing subscription platform and back it up with a 4–5 email dunning sequence.

On a $40,000/month business, recovering even 15% of involuntary churn returns several thousand dollars immediately. It’s the fastest, most provable win we deliver and always one of the first things we build.

What We Configure
  • ✓ Automated card-retry logic (days 1, 3, 5 & 7)
  • ✓ 4–5 email dunning sequence
  • ✓ Subscriber self-service card update flow
  • ✓ Dunning performance tracking & reporting
🔗

05

Referral & UGC Programs

We build two owned assets that keep producing subscribers regardless of what happens to paid platforms. First, a UGC system that emails active subscribers to share unboxing content in exchange for a small reward — feeding both organic discovery and the paid ad creative pipeline. Second, a referral program that gives subscribers a unique link and rewards successful referrals.

For pet and hobby boxes, mature referral programs generate 20–30% of new subscribers. It is the lowest cost-per-acquisition channel available and most brands have never turned it on properly.

What We Build
  • ✓ Referral program activation & reward structure
  • ✓ UGC collection email system
  • ✓ Content pipeline feeding paid ad creative
  • ✓ Referral performance tracking
  • ✓ Subscriber advocacy activation emails

Uses your existing platform features where possible. Runs permanently once built.

📊

06

Reporting & Optimisation

Each month you receive a one-page performance report covering the metrics that actually matter to your business: new subscribers acquired through agency channels, churn vs the prior month, change in monthly recurring revenue, revenue recovered through the dunning sequence, paid-channel acquisition cost, and lifetime-value trend.

This report doubles as the proof of value that supports the revenue-share calculation. We use the same data to continuously optimise — pausing underperforming ads, improving email performance, testing landing page variations, and adjusting the onboarding sequence wherever subscribers are churning earliest.

Metrics We Track
  • ✓ New subscribers via agency channels
  • ✓ Monthly churn rate vs prior month
  • ✓ Monthly recurring revenue change
  • ✓ Revenue recovered via dunning sequence
  • ✓ Paid-channel cost per acquisition
  • ✓ Subscriber lifetime value trend

Delivered monthly. Used to drive continuous optimisation across all six services.
